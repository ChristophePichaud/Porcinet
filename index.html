Our Story

Crafting Love Stories for the Screen

Founded in the heart of France, Porcinet is a premium film production company dedicated to the art of romantic cinema. We believe every great love story deserves to be told with passion, authenticity, and beauty.

From sun-soaked beaches to the cobblestone streets of Paris and London, our films explore the full spectrum of human emotion — desire, tenderness, heartbreak, and joy.

Every frame is a poem. Every story, a gift.
