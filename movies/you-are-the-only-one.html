You Are the Only One

2024 116 min Romance / Drama ★ 8.7 16+

After years of prioritising career over love, marine biologist Adèle meets Raphaël — a wildlife photographer whose quiet intensity mirrors her own. As they travel remote coastlines together, documenting the world's hidden wonders, they realise the greatest wonder of all is what grows between them.

Synopsis

Marine biologist Adèle has dedicated her life to the ocean's secrets. When she's assigned a photographer for her latest research expedition, she expects a distraction — instead she discovers Raphaël, a man who sees the world with the same depth and wonder as she does.

Across wild coastlines and remote islands, You Are the Only One tells the deeply moving story of two people who, in searching the world for beauty, find it at last in each other.
