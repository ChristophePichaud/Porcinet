Love in Paris

2024 112 min Romance / Drama ★ 8.5 16+

Camille returns to Paris ten years after a devastating love affair, determined to put the past to rest. But the city of light has other plans. A chance reunion on a rain-soaked bridge reignites something she had buried deep — and forces her to choose between the safety of forgetting and the exquisite danger of loving again.

Synopsis

A decade after a painful end to her great love, photographer Camille returns to Paris on assignment. She expects to find ghosts — instead she finds Étienne, her former lover, changed and tender and living just blocks from the Marais apartment they once shared.

Set against the timeless beauty of Paris — its cafés, gardens, and golden boulevards — Love in Paris is a story of forgiveness, longing, and the courage it takes to open your heart a second time.
