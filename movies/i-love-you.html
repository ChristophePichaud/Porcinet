I Love You

2023 108 min Romance / Drama ★ 8.4 16+

Three words that change everything. Set over a single transformative year, I Love You follows novelist Lena and musician Sébastien as they navigate the exhilarating and terrifying landscape of falling deeply in love — the raw vulnerability, the breathtaking joy, and the quiet moments that matter most.

Synopsis

Novelist Lena has written about love for years without truly experiencing it. When she meets Sébastien at a rainy bookshop opening, something shifts. Their relationship unfolds in four seasons — each bringing its own tests of trust, desire, and what it truly means to say those three irreversible words.

Intimate, lyrical, and deeply felt, I Love You is a film for anyone who has ever been brave enough to love with their whole heart.
