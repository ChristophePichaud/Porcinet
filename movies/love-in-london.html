Love in London

2023 98 min Romance / Drama ★ 8.0 16+

Amid London's grand architecture and misty mornings, French architect Élodie arrives for a six-month commission. She expects grey skies and professional distance — not the warm, sharp-witted James, her project collaborator. As they restore a Victorian townhouse together, they discover that hearts, like old buildings, can be beautifully rebuilt.

Synopsis

When Parisian architect Élodie accepts a challenging commission in London, she expects to focus entirely on her work. Instead, she meets James — dry-witted, brilliant, and annoyingly attractive — and they are assigned to restore a crumbling Victorian townhouse together. Sparks fly as freely as the plaster dust.

Filmed across London's most iconic neighbourhoods, Love in London is a warmly romantic story about two very different people discovering that opposites don't just attract — they complete each other.
