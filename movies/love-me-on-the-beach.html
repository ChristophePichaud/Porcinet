Love Me on the Beach

2024 104 min Romance / Drama ★ 8.2 16+

Under the endless blue skies of the Mediterranean, two strangers collide at the water's edge. Léa, a travel writer seeking solitude, and Marco, a free-spirited local fisherman, find themselves drawn together by the rhythm of the tides. Synopsis

Under the endless blue skies of the Mediterranean, travel writer Léa seeks quiet solitude after a difficult heartbreak. She finds instead Marco — sun-bronzed, kind-eyed, and impossibly charming. Their encounter at the shoreline sparks something electric. Over long summer days and warm, starlit nights, their connection grows from playful conversation into something neither can name nor deny.

Shot entirely on location in the South of France and Sardinia, Love Me on the Beach is a tender, sensual portrait of two people learning to trust love again.
