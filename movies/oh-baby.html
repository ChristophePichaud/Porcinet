Oh Baby

2024 95 min Romance / Comedy Drama ★ 8.1 16+

Clara never planned on falling in love — least of all with her new neighbour, the infuriating and irresistible Théo, who leaves his music too loud and his smile too bright. But life has a way of rewriting our plans. What starts as a neighbourhood dispute blossoms into something neither of them could have scripted — or imagined living without.

Synopsis

Clara, a no-nonsense pastry chef, has carefully constructed a quiet life — until Théo moves in next door with his guitar, his infectious laugh, and his complete disregard for her carefully maintained peace. Their rivalry escalates through a series of increasingly absurd confrontations, until one rainy evening changes everything.

Playful, warm, and genuinely moving, Oh Baby is a romantic comedy about what happens when the person who drives you crazy turns out to be the person who makes you whole.
